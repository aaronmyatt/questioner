Anti-flicker via PRELOAD (no animation): instead of cross-fading between pages, prerender the likely next pages so a click is an INSTANT swap of already-built content — no white-flash, no fade to find nauseating. Speculation Rules prerender renders the target page in the background; activation on click is near-instant. eagerness:eager prerenders all matching same-origin links as soon as the rules parse — fine here, the site is tiny. (Dial back to "moderate" = prerender on hover, or "conservative" = on pointerdown, if eager prerendering ever feels wasteful.) Browser support: Chromium (Chrome/Edge) only. Safari and Firefox ignore the rules and fall back to normal navigation — no regression, just no speed-up. For cross-browser coverage you'd add instant.page or .
